## 1. Diagnosis (the real problem, ≤3 sentences)

Algeria's ~3M+ SMBs are digitally unsophisticated, invisible on Google Maps (most
GBPs unclaimed or empty), and run their business on WhatsApp + Facebook — yet are
served by almost no agencies; the few that exist are human-powered, expensive
(150k–400k DZD/mo), and slow (hours-to-days response). The structural lever: AI
agents deliver local visibility + lead response at ~zero marginal cost, so BAZ can
own the local-growth layer at a price, speed, and 24/7 cadence no human agency can
match. The wedge: one focused, agent-native, measurable aspect — Maps rank +
WhatsApp response — turns local searches into booked customers the competition
literally cannot answer fast enough to win.

## 4. Architecture (channels, funnel, KPIs)

**Channels:** Google Business Profile (wedge) → WhatsApp Business API (conversion)
→ reviews (flywheel) → Facebook/Instagram lead-ads (ascension, client-funded).

**Funnel (Brunson hook-story-offer / Deiss journey):**
1. Prospector agent → finds unclaimed/empty GBPs by wilaya+vertical, screenshots
   the gap ("your business is missing from Maps — here's proof"), cold WhatsApp/DM.
2. 14-day free pilot → agent claims + optimizes GBP + turns on WhatsApp responder.
3. Ranked GBP → searchers call / direction-request / DM → responder <60s → books
   appointment → routes to owner to close.
4. Review agent → solicits 5★ post-booking → responds to reviews in-language →
   higher rank → more leads (flywheel).
5. Ascension → once Maps won + cash flowing, upsell Meta lead-ads (client budget)
   → full local lead engine; later a CRM + the BAZ Core/Growth tiers for the few
   who graduate.

**KPIs (Hopkins — revenue not vanity):**
| KPI | target (30d) | floor |
|---|---|---|
| GBP map-pack rank (city+kw) | top 3 | top 5 |
| GBP calls + directions + website / mo | +40% vs baseline | +15% |
| WhatsApp first-response time | <60s, 24/7 | <5 min |
| lead → booked rate | ≥35% | ≥20% |
| client retention | ≥9 mo | ≥6 mo |
| BAZ gross margin / client | ≥85% (agent-only) | ≥70% |

**Agent stack (the "solely by agents" layer — execution is 100% agents):**
| agent | owns |
|---|---|
| Prospector | finds empty GBPs by wilaya+vertical; drafts Darija/FR outreach |
| GBP-Optimizer | claim, categories, photos, weekly trilingual posts, Q&A, citations |
| Responder | WhatsApp + FB/IG DM, <60s, books via calendar, escalates to owner |
| Review | solicits post-booking, responds in-language |
| Reporter | weekly DZD-impact report (calls, DMs, bookings, rank) in Darija/FR |
| (human) QC | ≤15 min/client/wk: approves GBP claim, first outreach, weekly report — tapers to spot-checks as agents self-prove |

> "Solely by agents" = fulfillment/delivery is 100% agent-run. The only humans are
> the BAZ orchestrator (strategy + final sales close + oversight) and the client
> owner (does the 2-min GBP verification + closes the booked appointment / delivers
> the service). QC is a taper, not a delivery role — not a junior pod.
