# 🏢 Agency Business Model

## The Business
We are a **marketing agency**. We sell outcomes, not tools.

## What We Sell (Services)

| Service | Description | Deliverable |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **QR Solutions** | Custom QR landing pages for restaurants, retail, services | Branded QR page (powered by QrCreator) |
| **Web Design** | Landing pages, vitrines, mini-sites | Deployed website |
| **SEO** | Search visibility, local SEO, content optimization | Rankings + reports |
| **Social Media** | Content calendars, management, growth | Content + analytics |
| **Branding** | Logo, identity, brand guidelines | Brand kit |
| **Content** | Blog posts, copy, email campaigns | Written content |
| **Email Marketing** | Campaigns, newsletters, automation | Sent campaigns + metrics |

## How QR Fits In
QrCreator is a **commodity** — a tool we use to deliver QR solutions to clients:

```
CLIENT NEEDS                    WE DELIVER
─────────────                   ──────────
Restaurant wants digital menu → Custom QR menu page (QrCreator) + printed QR cards
Shop wants online presence    → Vitrine QR page (QrCreator) + social media setup
Business wants NFC cards      → Branded NFC cards + QR landing page
```

The QR code is the **hook**. The **retainer** is the monthly marketing service wrapped around it.

## Revenue Model

### One-Time Projects
- QR landing page setup: 5,000–15,000 DZD
- Website/vitrine: 15,000–50,000 DZD
- Brand kit: 10,000–30,000 DZD

### Monthly Retainers (Bread & Butter)
- Basic (1 service): 10,000–20,000 DZD/month
- Growth (2-3 services): 25,000–50,000 DZD/month
- Premium (full stack): 50,000–100,000 DZD/month

### Upsells
- Extra QR pages
- Social media posts
- SEO blog content
- Email campaigns
- NFC card reorders

## Service Delivery Flow
```
Lead → Discovery Call → Proposal → Contract → Onboarding →
Deliver → Report → Upsell → Retain
```
