## CFO Information

**No CFO information was found for any of the agencies.** This is typical for Algerian SMEs where the founder/CEO often handles financial matters, or the role is not publicly disclosed. Financial roles in Algerian agencies are typically:

- **Founder/CEO** handles finances directly
- **External accountant** (comptable) handles bookkeeping — not a public role
- **No dedicated CFO** until agencies reach 20+ employees

**Only KYO Conseil (58 people)** might have a formal CFO role, but it's not publicly listed.
