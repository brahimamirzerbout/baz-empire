# Social Media Marketing

SMM = using social platforms to connect with your audience to build brand,
increase sales, and drive traffic — via publishing, engagement, listening, and
paid social ads.

## The #1 principle: focus, don't spray

- **Pick 1–2 primary platforms** where your audience actually spends time. A
  dominant presence on 2 beats a mediocre presence on 5–6.
- Random acts of content lead to random results. Strategy first.
- Use the **"rule of two"**: one **discovery** platform (TikTok, Reels, Shorts,
  Pinterest) + one **relationship** platform (LinkedIn, Threads, Reddit, email).

## The 7-step strategy cycle (Buffer)

1. Set **SMART goals**.
2. Define your **target audience**.
3. Choose **content pillars** (repeatable themes).
4. Choose your **platforms** (where audience is).
5. Build a **content calendar**.
6. **Analyze** performance.
7. **Evaluate** and iterate (it's a flywheel, not a one-time plan).

## Content pillars & ratio

- Most successful brands use a **70/30 value-to-promo** split (or 60/25/15:
  60% educational, 25% personal/brand, 15% promotional).
- A working pillar set = 4–6 pillars, each with a fixed output ratio, format, and
  owner. Example: 40% educate, 30% entertain, 20% inspire, 10% promote.
- **UGC (user-generated content)** can see ~8.7x higher engagement than
  brand-produced content in some verticals — audiences trust peers.

## Platform notes (2026 benchmarks)

- **TikTok** — best organic reach (~5.3% engagement). Vertical 9:16, hook in
  first 2 sec, trending audio, authenticity over polish. Strong for under-35 B2C.
- **LinkedIn** — ~6.5% organic engagement; **personal posts get ~561% more reach
  than company pages**. Best for B2B. Long-form text, document carousels, native
  video, thoughtful comments.
- **Instagram** — Reels + carousels; strongest all-around for visual/e-commerce.
- **YouTube** — long-form builds authority; Shorts drive subscriber growth.
- **Facebook** — organic reach ~0.06% for pages (pay-to-play); use for groups,
  local ads, Marketplace.
- **Pinterest** — high-quality visual, aspirational/visual products.

## Engagement is the real lever

- Replying to comments consistently raises engagement across platforms (up to
  +42% on Threads, +30% on LinkedIn per Buffer's 2026 report).
- Answer DMs < 60 min (business hours), comments < 2 hr.
- Treat DMs/comments as a product surface, not an afterthought.

## Organic + paid = one system

- Pure organic plateaus; pure paid builds no equity. Use organic to find winners,
  paid to amplify them, creators to extend reach.
- **Whitelisted creator content** (ads run from the creator's handle) often
  outperforms brand creative 2–4x on CTR/CPA on Meta & TikTok.
- Typical mid-market split: 50% paid to cold, 20% retargeting, 20% creators,
  10% experimentation. Review quarterly vs blended CAC.

## Measurement (3 layers)

- **Leading**: saves, shares, watch-time, DM volume, comment velocity, branded
  search. Predicts results 60–90 days out.
- **Lagging**: follower growth rate, reach %, engagement rate per pillar, top
  posts by format (report vs 90-day median, ignore viral spikes).
- **Business**: branded search growth, assisted conversions, social-sourced
  pipeline, CAC contribution, LTV differential. Report as a range; last-click
  undervalues social.
- **Server-side conversion APIs are mandatory** (Meta CAPI, TikTok Events API,
  LinkedIn Conversions API) — client-side pixels miss 30–60% post iOS 17 / ITP.
  Run geo-lift/holdout tests at least annually.

## Beginner start

Pick one platform, set one clear goal, post useful content, respond to people,
measure what resonates, then expand. Progress > perfection.
