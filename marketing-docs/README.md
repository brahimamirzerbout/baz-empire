## How the channels fit together

The dominant theme across every source: **integration**. No single channel wins
alone. A strong system looks like this:

- **Owned media** (site, blog, email list) — assets you control and that compound.
- **Earned media** (press, shares, reviews, word of mouth) — highest credibility.
- **Paid media** (search/social/display ads) — fast, controllable, stops when budget stops.

SEO feeds content, content fuels social and email, and paid amplifies the best of it.

## The customer funnel (applied digitally)

1. **Awareness** — SEO, social, paid ads.
2. **Consideration** — content, email, reviews.
3. **Conversion** — landing pages, offers, checkout.
4. **Retention & loyalty** — email, community, post-purchase experience.
