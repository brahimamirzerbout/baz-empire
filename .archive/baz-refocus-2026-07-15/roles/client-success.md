# Part 9 — The Missing Seat: Client Success Lead

The org chart says it plainly: **Client Success / Account Director — MISSING.**
Founder-direct account management does not scale past you, and it is why you
can't sleep. This is not optional growth; it's the thing that lets the business
exist without you in the room.

## The role (hire or promote before client #5)
- **Owns:** the client relationship post-sale, onboarding, monthly results review,
  renewal, and upsell of the 13 hidden services (once a client trusts the first win).
- **Does NOT own:** delivery. Senior specialists deliver. This person protects the
  client from founder burnout and protects the founder from being the bottleneck.
- **Single KPI:** net revenue retention. A renewed client is the only proof.

## Why now, not later
The `bazventures-org-chart.md` says "add before scaling beyond ~5 active clients."
You are at the edge of that. Adding this seat turns 14 services into a renewal
engine instead of a founder to-do list.

## Spec (for the posting)
- 5+ yrs client/account leadership at a senior-only or boutique shop.
- Comfortable saying no to scopes that break the one-offer rule (Part 2).
- Writes the monthly results note clients actually read.
- Reports to founder; owns the renewal number.

## In code
This role is reflected in `assets/positioning.ts` is intentionally absent — the
positioning file describes the *market* face (one audience, one offer). The seat
is an *internal* hire. Track it in the org chart, not the homepage.
