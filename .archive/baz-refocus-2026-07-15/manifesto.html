Intensity
beats extensity.

Every time. One audience, deeply known. One channel, mastered. One offer, sharpened until it cuts. Go deep, not wide — or don't bother.

This is not a preference. It's a law of focus.

Marketing fails when it's spread. Five channels touched beat none mastered. We chose the one.

Extensity (what we reject)

Seven platforms at once. Ten services on the homepage. A budget sliced so thin it buys noise. Being everywhere means being nowhere.

Intensity (what we do)

One persona, understood to the bone. One channel, owned at the algorithm level. One hero piece a week so good it travels without being pushed.

The rule of one

One audience to start. One channel to dominate. One offer to push. One hero creative per cycle. One KPI to optimize. One funnel to perfect. Master the one — then, and only then, add the second.

This principle lives in docs/INTENSITY_PRINCIPLE.md. We stopped keeping it internal.
