## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does a performance agency do?

Performance agency optimizes marketing outcomes based on key performance indicators, e.g. increase sales, minimize attrition, etc. They focus on data and analytics and tend to specialize in channels such as search engine marketing, affiliate marketing, various social media platforms, etc. Clients should expect quantitative reporting that are tied to business metrics.

### Is SEO a performance marketing?

SEO refers to search engine optimization. It is absolutely a part of performance services. This practice increases a business's visibility on search engine result pages (SERP) and, therefore, its online visibility to potential clients, ultimately leading to better brand awareness and higher lead generation.

### What is the role of a performance marketing associate?

A performance marketing associate is often an individual contributor who specializes in a specific performance channel, e.g. SEO, SEM, social media platforms, paid search, CRM, etc. Key skills include a good understanding of marketing analytics, problem-solving using data, ability to collaborate with both businesses and technical teams.

### Digital Agencies VS Performance Agencies

A digital agency broadly refers to any agency that utilizes digital marketing channels. Given that modern marketing is increasingly digital, it has become a marketing agency's nomenclature.  
A digital agency can be a performance agency, but it can also be a specialty agency that focuses on creative services, branding, etc. These digital services ultimately should deliver on specific business needs. However, a performance agency is more directly managing channels and services that are linked to KPIs, such as affiliate marketing and search engine marketing.

### Branding Agencies VS Performance Agencies

A branding agency and a performance agency have different focuses.  
A branding agency concentrates on developing the identity of an organization or business through a range of services, including creating a unique identity and logo, conducting market research and customer surveys to understand the target audience, and then segmenting and profiling the target market, defining the most relevant marketing messages and copywriting, etc.  
A performance agency rather focuses on measurable marketing channels. These can include most digital channels, but could also include the more traditional direct mail. They use data to achieve pre-defined outcomes, should it be increasing sales or profit, or maximizing outreach.

### What to look for in an agency specialized in performance marketing?

Find the agency that suits your company and marketing goals, by looking at the following key criteria:  
1\. Service & Support a. Does the agency offer multi-channel support and services?  
b. Is there a wealth of strategy & experience available to you?  
c. Core team- is there enough experience in the core team and do you have access to senior staff?  
d. Support team- who does it consist of to provide you with the high quality work you need?  
e. What is staff turnover like and will this impact your work?  
2\. Track Record: look at- b. Client testimonials c. Results-based case studies 3. Added Value: evaluate their- a. Certifications & accreditation b. Other digital services c. Other network or media partnerships that could benefit you.

### How much do companies spend on digital marketing?

Companies typically budget for 5 - 15% of their total revenue towards marketing. This will usually be lower for companies with a higher cost of certain goods, such as construction or manufacturing companies. While service related companies, such as technology or SaaS companies, will typically budget more toward marketing. Companies spend approximately 50% or more of this marketing budget on digital marketing.
