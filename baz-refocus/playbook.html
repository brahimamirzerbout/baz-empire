The rule of one

Prove it on one channel. Then add the second.

Spread budgets buy noise, not data. This 90‑day cycle has exactly one audience, one channel, one offer, one KPI.

One audience

Technical founders (Part 1).

One channel

Founder-led email + LinkedIn (owned + earned).

one offer

"Your marketing system in 45 days."

one KPI

Calls booked from the list — not impressions.

The sequence

  • Week 1 — Stand up the permission asset. Goal: 100 subscribers.
  • Week 2–4 — One remarkable essay/week to email + LinkedIn. Nothing else.
  • Week 5–8 — Double down on the format that got the most replies. Kill the rest.
  • Week 9–12 — One paid test on the proven message, enough spend for significance in days.

What we do NOT do

  • No TikTok / Meta / Google Ads / podcast until the primary channel compounds.
  • No blog-spam, no 6-platform cross-post, no "be everywhere."
  • No second offer. The 13 hidden services stay hidden.

The number that matters

calls_booked_from_list / subscribers. If it moves, the business works. If not, we change the message — not the channel count.
