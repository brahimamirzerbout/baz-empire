# Part 4 — Park the Side Projects

The Dip is real: most projects die because no one pushes through the hard middle.
You cannot push through five at once. Park all but the one with a real customer.

## Rule
One active bet. Everything else is explicitly **paused** and dated, so it's a
decision, not drift.

## How to un-park
A parked project may be promoted to ACTIVE only when ALL are true:
1. The agency has a waitlist (proves the founder's time is the constraint).
2. A client-success lead owns renewals (Part 9).
3. The project has one named customer who paid or committed.

Otherwise: leave it parked. Intensity beats extensity.
