# Part 8 — The One-Channel Playbook

Prove the model on ONE channel before touching a second. Per `INTENSITY_PRINCIPLE.md`,
spread budgets produce noise, not data.

## The Rule of One (this 90-day cycle)
- **One audience** — technical founders (Part 1).
- **one channel** — founder-led email + LinkedIn (owned + earned).
- **one offer** — "Your marketing system in 45 days" (Part 2).
- **one hero creative per week** — one essay so good it travels without paid push.
- **one KPI** — replies & calls booked from the list. Not impressions. Not followers.

## The sequence
1. **Week 1** — Stand up the permission asset (Part 6). Zero subscribers → goal: 100.
2. **Week 2–4** — One remarkable essay/week. Publish to email + LinkedIn. No other channel.
3. **Week 5–8** — Double down on the format/angle that got the most replies. Kill the rest.
4. **Week 9–12** — One paid test (LinkedIn) ONLY on the proven message, enough daily
   spend to reach significance in days. Expand ONLY after the channel compounds.

## What we do NOT do (extensity)
- No TikTok, no Meta, no Google Ads, no podcast — until the primary channel compounds.
- No blog-for-SEO spam, no 6-platform cross-post, no "be everywhere" thinking.
- No second offer. The 13 hidden services stay hidden.

## The number that matters
`calls_booked_from_list / subscribers`. If that moves, the business works. If it
doesn't, we change the message — not the channel count.
