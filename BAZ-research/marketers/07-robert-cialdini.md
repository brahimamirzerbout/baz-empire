## The 7 Principles of Persuasion [fact]

1. **Reciprocity** — people say yes if they owe you. Give first (value, insight, audit).
2. **Commitment & Consistency** — small yeses lead to big yeses. Align the ask with what
   they've already said/done, especially publicly.
3. **Social Proof** — people say yes if others like them are saying yes. Show the crowd.
4. **Authority** — people say yes to established authority. Demonstrate expertise
   specifically, not generically.
5. **Liking** — people say yes to people they like. Be genuine, relatable, similar.
6. **Scarcity** — people say yes to what's rare/dwindling. Only use genuine scarcity.
7. **Unity** (added 2016) — people say yes to those who share identity/goals/values.
   "We" not "you and me."

Plus **Pre-Suasion**: what you present *before* the message frames how it's received.
Direct attention to the lever you want pulled.

## Cialdini Code of Ethics [fact]

Be truthful · forgo manipulation of people/facts · use the principle that *already exists
naturally* in the situation · use what's wise for all concerned · never injure the
relationship · inform (educate) into agreement · relevant contrasts only · own mistakes
ASAP ("but").

## BAZ application

- **Reciprocity = the paid diagnostic.** BAZ gives a real 5-day audit (value) before
  asking for a retainer. The "if we don't find a leak worth 10x, you don't pay" is
  reciprocity + risk reversal in one.
- **Authority through specificity** (Hopkins convergence): BAZ doesn't claim "we're
  experts" — it demonstrates by shipping the tracking stack and naming the leak in
  dollars. Specificity = authority trigger.
- **Social proof:** BAZ's case studies with real numbers (replaced composites) are
  load-bearing social proof. Use segment-matched proof (a B2B SaaS prospect needs a
  B2B SaaS case, not a DTC one).
- **Unity = the operator tribe.** BAZ's category-of-one ("the operator who ships")
  creates Unity — "we're people who ship, not people who deck." Hormozi's
  us-vs-them positioning is Unity applied at scale.
- **Pre-Suasion in proposals:** BAZ presents value, proof, and the diagnostic *before*
  price ever appears. Frame the conversation (instrumented growth) before the ask.
- **Ethics guardrail (non-negotiable):** BAZ uses only principles that *already exist
  naturally* in the situation. Never manufacture false scarcity, fake social proof, or
  manipulative authority. This is the Cialdini Code + BAZ's "never deceptive" guardrail,
  the same rule.
