# 02 — Outreach sequence (cold email + LinkedIn)

> Copy-mode. Passed the market/offer lenses before a word was written:
> - **Schwartz**: buyer is *solution-aware* (knows they waste ad spend, doesn't
>   know the fix) → lead with the mechanism, not the problem.
> - **Kennedy/Hormozi**: offer = a free 20-min ROAS audit (risk-reversed,
>   time-boxed, no deck). The "grand-slam" angle: they see their own numbers
>   reallocated live.
> - **Wiebe**: CTA matches intent ("see where your budget is leaking", not
>   "book a call").
>
> Voice: senior, precise, no hype. Short load-bearing sentences. One ask.

## Rules of engagement

| Rule | Why |
|---|---|
| **Never send a deck.** The demo is the live engine on their data. | positioning, close rate |
| **Never quote a price in cold outreach.** Price lands after the audit reveals the gap. | close rate |
| **One ask per touch.** The ask is always the 20-min audit. | lead flow |
| **Reply within 4 hours to any response.** Speed = signal of senior operators. | close rate |
| **No follow-up after the break-up unless they reply.** Respect the "no." | positioning |
| **Log every touch in the CRM (hub, when wired).** No touch untracked. | lead flow, scalability |
| **MENA prospects: AR or FR variant available on request.** Don't default to EN in Algiers. | geography calibration |
